Corporate Proposal | PELITA

Transformed a standard, text-heavy administrative document into a high-impact corporate sponsorship proposal. My role was to bridge the gap between technical data and visual storytelling—converting raw program details into a professionally structured multi-page layout. By integrating custom charts, thematic imagery, and a cohesive brand identity, I created a compelling visual asset designed specifically to secure program funding and acquisition.

The Challenge

The challenge stem from the requirement from client to visualize the raw details; which mostly in written form, into a proper graphs and graphics that suitable. This also alongside the need to keep all the details concise and packed.

The Solution

Within the design process, I start with structuring the corporate proposal design into segments first. From the segmentation, i determined which details fit in which segments. Furhtermore, i also remove any redundant & repetitive information to prevent unnecessary information overload. After such action, i start recontextualize the infromation; making it more concise, and shorter, while utilizing graphical elements and iconography wherever suitable to limit text over use.

The Outcome

Beautifully designed corporate proposal booklet. Suitable for internal memo and meeting. Complete with all the details needed without the redundency often come with the common documentaion format.

Paranoid | A Short Story

Visit Project ↗

Paranoid is a short story porject for our course in class assignment. It was created as our final semester submission; to determined our understanding of visual effect in video editing at our beginner level experience.

The Challenge

Back when this project started, the entire team only has basic rudimentary skill in video editing; especially using fast, quick editing software such as CapCut, and any other "drag-and-drop" editing suite. The requirement of special visual effect does pose a challenge for us on what we going to create, let alone how to create it.

The Solution

After Effect was the clear choice for creating all the visual effect in this video. Being a robust and powerful tools, we rapidly perform the pre-production, plan the shots, shooting the footages, and collect it in a unified archive for access. The next step is for colour calibration and adjustment to create a consistent colour reproduction throughout muktiple shots. A mixed use of plugins, as well as the old-school roto brush in the project prove a satisfactory result.

The Outcome

The result, is a short story video project, that created with the fundimental of cinematography applied, along side sufficient use of prefessional effect that perfectly capture the tone and direction this project going for.

Game Synopsis

The player assumes the role of a freshman recently accepted into UPSI. They quickly learn about the university's most sought-after organization: the STEM club, which is notoriously selective. The club is hosting its annual "Campus Derby," an RC racing championship that spans all of the university's campuses. Winning this competition is the player's ticket to gaining membership and proving their engineering and driving prowess. All in the same time, the player meets her old friends turned nemesis in the championship. The protagonist had to compete for the STEM club membership all while uncovering her old history.
